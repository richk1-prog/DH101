Week 9 Reflection

Core Question

What is creativity when AI can generate endlessly? Where is the human in generative work?

Creativity even in the age of AI is what it always has partially been, the human mind, AI may be able to create its own works endlessly but without a human mind to come up with ideas so absurd or vivid eventually there would be no creativity but so long as human imagination persists even with AI creativity would not change. In my bot I had the most control over the output, when I was writing the prompt, and coming up with the idea of what I wanted to get out of it, when I was deciding how it should look, and adjusting the prompt to get what I was looking for.
