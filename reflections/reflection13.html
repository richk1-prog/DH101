Week 13 Reflection

Core Question

Do we imagine AI futures as utopian, dystopian, or something in between? What do those visions reveal about us?

I imagine an in-between where AI's do work alongside human, but don't replace human labor, where truly nothing big changes about life, apart from AI becomes a tool for workers to utilize. I think what my vision reveals about us is that in current modern day is that our current use of AI is as a tool and that I cannot see it as something be used beyond being a tool for us to utilize.
