Week 10 Reflection

Core Question

How does AI change our relationship to play, rules, and narrative?

AI changes how we view play, and rules as it becomes less about doing everything only a few ways, and instead about trying to see if we can play other ways and see if we can alter or make new rules to play, all with the intent of matching or following along with whatever narrative has already been made, but with our own way of playing with that story and rules.
