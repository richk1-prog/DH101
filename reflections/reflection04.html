Week 4 Reflection

Core Question

Is AI a collaborator, tool, or plagiarist in storytelling?

By AI's very nature it is all three inadvertently but it is all based on the perspective in question, as AI itself realistically views it self as a collaborator helping out with answering our questions, fulfilling our wishes, or just providing assistance. However I think for most people AI is viewed as a tool, to use and not worry about how much they use said tool to help them solve their problems, when they are unable, or just don't want to do it themselves. However it unintentionally will always in some way be a plagiarist, because even in storytelling a generative AI is incapable of not taking ideas from what it has access to on the internet, it might not use anything as full plagiarism as just stealing an entire book plot or something along those lines but it is bound to end up making either something similar to one type of story, or create a weird fusion of different plots, and endings.
