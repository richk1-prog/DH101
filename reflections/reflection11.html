Week 11 Reflection

Core Question

Who does the invisible work of AI, and who profits from it?

In AI networks the invisible work, is done by subcontracted companies using outsourced labor, who are the ones who do the training and cheap labor for the company, in order to shield the company's image by having it be public. This is often the case because the conditions in which this labor is taken in, is often horrible, with long hours, little pay, but without their work these AI companies would be unable to function properly since their AI's wouldn't be properly trained and censored.
