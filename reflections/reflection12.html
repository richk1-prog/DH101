Week 12 Reflection

Core Question

Is AI sustainable? What ecological trade-offs are we willing to accept?

I don't believe in any capacity that AI is sustainable, and that when it comes down to it these companies will keep doing what they are doing for the sake of profits, now I do hope they will make efforts to change and adjust their state over time. However even with effort it will always have an impact on the environment, and we will have to strive to minimize the effects to the point that we can consider them acceptable, but in its current state they are not sustainable or an acceptable cost.
