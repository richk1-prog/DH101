Week 2 Reflection

Core Question

Can AI help us define what it means to be human, or does it reduce us to patterns?

AI is incapable of defining what it means to be human, because its very being is to use patterns based off what others say, and part of being human is that people have their own thoughts and will never truly come to the complete same conclusion. The very system of AI is to search the internet or anything it has access to and discover a pattern to base its opinion on. AI itself is reduced to patterns only because that is how it was designed, AI doesn't reduce us to patterns, so long as people do not take AI's word as fact, instead view it as at most a suggestion.
