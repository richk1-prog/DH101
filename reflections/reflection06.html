Week 6 Reflection

Core Question

What do we gain/lose when machines "read" literature for us?

When we let AI's read, summarize and give us meanings from literature we, can gain a chance to view the literature from possibly a lens other than how we would normally view it if the opinion does not correlate with our own interpretation or understanding of something. While we often lose that sense of critical thinking when machines provide us with their idea of the important details and lose the allure of what happens in books, or of the information that is held in it, by shortsighted artificial understandings on the contents of literature.
