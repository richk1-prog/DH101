# Week 2 Reflection

## Core Question
Can AI help us define what it means to be human, or does it reduce us to patterns?

## Key Concepts to Consider
- Essential vs. constructed aspects of humanity
- How AI identifies and replicates patterns
- The limits of pattern-based understanding
- Reductionism vs. complexity

## Guiding Questions
- What aspects of humanity can be captured in patterns? What escapes them?
- Does studying how AI mimics humanity reveal something about us?
- Are we more than patterns, or are patterns essential to understanding us?
- How does this relate to your own sense of what makes you human?
