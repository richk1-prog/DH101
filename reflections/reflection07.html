Week 7 Reflection

Core Question

How does AI reshape global geographies of power, labor, and environment?

AI impacts global geographies through, labor, and the environment by concentrating technological power in a few countries that are not necessarily big, and major tech companies that control data, infrastructure, and innovation. This shifts global labor patterns as some jobs become automated while new technology-focused jobs grow in specific regions, mainly ones with less labor laws, or restrictions on work. At the same time, AI systems rely on large data centers that consume significant energy and water, creating environmental struggles in these places where they are being developed and operated on.
