Make 3 — Week 03

Comic (Image by Google Gemini, Script by Me)

AI Prompt for the image (Google Gemini): Create a 6 panel comic from left to right. Panel 1: start with a big dog who already has a large amount of bones with a bunch of smaller dogs surrounding him, boasting how he has the most bones and has a blank speech bubble. Panel 2: show one small dog happy on its own with its own bone. Panel 3: show the big dog pointing off demanding the bone from the small dog with a blank speech bubble. Panel 4: show the smaller dogs taking the one bone from the lone small dog. Panel 5: the big dog boasting again with a blank speech bubble coming from it while the small dogs look at him expecting rewards Panel 6: Have the big dog look at them disgusted and give a speech bubble

Reflection: In the comic I wanted to show how often times in any hierarchical environment, with the larger already successful entity always wanting more, and not caring how they do it or what they give as compensation to their “subordinates”. However I didn’t want to risk the AI outputting the wrong idea, or putting panels out of order so I had to be clear about what each panel needed to have, and the order in which they should be placed. The comic format definitely helped in letting the audience AI focus on the image, going panel by panel, based on my instructions one after the other. The details in the dogs' faces then they think they'll be rewarded for aiding helps to show more of how people think the harder they work the more likely they are to be promoted, or given bonuses. However their reactions in the very next panel show how shocked and silently upset they are because they know they deserve the reward for putting in the work but are denied and told to work harder.
