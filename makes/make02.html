Original Image

Better Call Kian Made through (DALL·E via Chat-GPT) Prompt: Take this photo and make your own version of it in the style of Saul Goodman, and Better Call Saul style

Distorted AI image (Canva)

Reflection: In the AI image it most likely helped that I provided an image for it to use as a sample and basis for my request image, the AI decided to broaden my shoulders, applied mood lighting/ a more yellow filter, and appears to have increased the facial hair on my face. I feel like the AI thinks a normal person looks like everyone has some form of mood lighting on them, this might come from the fact that very often images on the internet carry some form of mood lighting in them. In the AI image they also adjusted my facial structure, but kept my hair mostly the same, it also gave me a more pronounced neck, and tilted my chin up. In general I mostly see myself in the hair, and forehead the rest feels edited heavily by AI. In the distorted image, obviously I inverted the image, and sharpened the image details so you can see more details in the suit Chat-GPT gave me. They also for some reason threw the logo from "Better Call Saul!" in and made the background look like a law office. While I did expect the AI to put a suit on and adjust the lighting I didn't expect the logo in the corner like it was an image linked to the show.
