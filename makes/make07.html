3. I think what my network chain shows is that power isn’t necessarily about how much of a resource you control, or how many resources you actually have access to yourself. I believe my map shows more that real power is more about how many connections you have between people, or how many allies you have backing you and helping you accumulate those resources. Since, while Open AI itself is only really supporting a company that uses AI’s to locate hidden mineral resources within the earth, and working closely with NVIDIA to acquire the electronic materials for their data centers. But the way that NVIDIA and KoBold Metals connect to these other groups and companies over the world shows how having these allies help Open AI have as much as they already do. I think for the most part the people who actually are visible are the large companies as a whole, but we don't ever actually see any workers within these companies, in part because if we did, then there is a chance we might as humans feel empathy for the potentially poor work conditions that are being used.
